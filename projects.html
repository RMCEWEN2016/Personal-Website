PROJECTS

I'm a tinkerer...

...and with tinkering comes with lots and lots of projects! Not everything I do is completely digital. One of the best things you can try out as a CAD student is 3D printing, they go hand in hand! The reason CAD is a thing is for manufacturing. Normally you would use metalworking machines to create these parts, but now a new technology has entered the market and they are getting cheaper and cheaper by the day! Seriously, you can buy a 3D printer for like $300 nowawdays.
This section is to showcase my endeavours and hobbies, as I'm always creating new things. Some of the things I do include 3D Printing (obviously), Metalworking (particularly Forging), and other random projects that help me do the things I want to do.
